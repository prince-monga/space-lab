Who are we

Our Vision

Our vision is to provide an affordable, accessible, and easy-to-use 3D printing and product development service that empowers people to create and customize products. We want to give people the ability to create anything they can imagine, and to do so without having to worry about the cost or complexity of traditional manufacturing methods. We aim to become the go-to provider of 3D printing and product development service.

    Why choose us

         Through years of research and development, we have developed state-of-the-art technology. We can print your model with variety of materials. We can work with most of designs regarding automotive industry, fashion industry and even the aerospace and civil industries. You can concentrate on the design and the program and leave the rest to us.

    High Quality Output

    3D printers are getting more and more precise, with even the cheapest machines showing impressive levels of precision. However, it’s not just the machines that contribute to the bottom line. People who work professionally with 3D printing make a significant contribution to producing a professional quality part. We have the knowledge and experience to advise you on the best way to prepare your model, choose the most suitable material and finally create the final part.

    Time consistency

    Delays are a very common problem in the manufacturing industry. It stems from the dependencies that service agencies have on various suppliers of tooling, raw materials. These vendors typically fail to meet their time commitments and this has a cascading impact on the entire schedule. This is also a very difficult issue for us, but we try to mitigate delays as best we can by maintaining stock levels and building closer relationships with our suppliers.

    End to End Solution

    The manufacturing requirements for new product development are very different from those of a mature product. When developing new products, the quantities are very small (between 1 and 10), which requires multiple manufacturing technologies such as CNC machining, vacuum casting, 3D printing, 3D scanning and other tasks like PCB designing, PCB assembly and manufacturing, Programming of microcontroller. Therefore, a one-stop-shop is very important as new businesses lack the bandwidth to deal with multiple vendors low volume orders.

    Color Variety and Finishing

    Wide Variety of Colors and Finishes with so many materials available, so you can match your product with your expectations. In addition to color, several surface finishing techniques can be used on 3D printed parts. For starters, 3D printed parts can be processed to completely hide layer visibility, making them look like they didn’t even come out of a 3D printer.
